Project Mission

Objectives & Goals

Our primary mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where technology bridges the gap between surplus food and hunger.

Reduce Food Waste

Minimize the massive daily food wastage in hotels by identifying surplus food and channeling it effectively before expiry.

Connect Stakeholders

Create a unified platform to seamlessly connect Food Donors (Hotels), NGOs, and Volunteers for rapid coordination.

Verified Distribution

Ensure safety and authenticity through a rigorous vetting process for all registered organizations and volunteers.

Real-time Visibility

Provide live tracking of food availability and delivery status to ensure timely pickups and prevent spoilage.

Secure Transactions

Implement OTP-based verification for handovers to maintain a secure audit trail of every donation made.
