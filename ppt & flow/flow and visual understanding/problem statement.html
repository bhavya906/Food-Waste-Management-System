The Challenge

Problem Statement

Despite surplus food production in the hospitality sector, a significant disconnect prevents this resource from reaching those in need.

Massive Food Wastage

Large hotels and restaurants discard vast amounts of edible food daily due to a lack of immediate disposal channels, contributing to environmental degradation.

Inefficient Connectivity

There is no centralized platform to connect donors with NGOs and volunteers in real-time. Communication gaps lead to missed donation opportunities.

Timing & Verification

Without a streamlined system, coordinating pickups before food spoils is difficult. Ensuring food safety and preventing misuse remains a major challenge.

Global Impact
