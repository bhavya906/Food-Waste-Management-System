Platform Architecture

System Overview

The Food Donation Management System is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to facilitate the seamless transfer of surplus food from donors to verified beneficiaries.

Core Functionality

Centralized Hub Acts as a single point of truth connecting disjointed stakeholders in the food supply chain.
Verification & Security Every transaction is authenticated via Admin approval and OTP verification mechanisms.
Real-Time Coordination Live status updates ensure perishable food is collected and delivered before expiry.

The system operates on a role-based access control (RBAC) model.
